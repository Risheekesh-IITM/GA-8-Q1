Weighted Average Cost of Capital

\[ WACC = \frac{E}{V}r_E + \frac{D}{V}r_D(1 - T_C) \]

  • E = Equity Value
  • D = Debt Value
  • V = E + D
  • r_E , r_D = returns on equity & debt
  • T_C = corporate tax
